Shipping Policy

We want to make sure that our customers receive their orders in a timely and efficient manner. That's why we have established a comprehensive shipping policy that covers various aspects of the shipping process. In this essay, we will discuss the key elements of our shipping policy.

We offer domestic shipping within South Africa. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and delivery times may vary depending on the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping takes 1-2 business days.

We also offer international shipping to our customers. Delivery times may vary depending on the destination country and the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard international shipping typically takes 7-21 business days, while expedited international shipping takes 3-5 business days.

Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties or taxes charged by the recipient's country. We are not responsible for any customs fees, duties, or taxes associated with international orders. It is the customer's responsibility to pay these fees, if applicable.

Shipping costs vary depending on the destination country and the shipping method selected at checkout. We offer free standard shipping within South Africa for orders over a certain amount. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout and may be subject to change without prior notice.

Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track your order's delivery status.

We currently do not ship to certain countries or regions due to legal or logistical restrictions. Please check our website for the most up-to-date information on shipping restrictions.

We strive to provide our customers with a smooth and efficient shopping experience. Our shipping policy reflects our commitment to timely delivery and customer satisfaction. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our shipping policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
