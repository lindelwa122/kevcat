Privacy Policy

KevCat is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use that information, and the measures we take to protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

When you make a purchase on our website, we collect certain personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address. We also collect information about your purchase history, including the products you have purchased and the dates of those purchases.

In addition, we may collect certain non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system. This information is used to improve the performance and functionality of our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to process your orders and to provide you with a better shopping experience. This includes communicating with you about your orders and providing you with information about our products and promotions. We may also use your information to conduct market research and to improve the functionality of our website.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to process your orders or as required by law. We may share your information with our trusted partners who provide services on our behalf, such as shipping and payment processing. These partners are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information very seriously. We use a variety of physical, electronic, and managerial measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We also use SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption to protect your personal information when it is transmitted over the internet.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites that are not affiliated with KevCat. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these websites before providing any personal information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal requirements. We will post any updates to our website and will notify you of any material changes to our privacy policy.

Conclusion

At KevCat, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. Our privacy policy reflects our commitment to transparency, security, and trust. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
